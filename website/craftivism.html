Craftivism

What is it?

Creativity and art is a great way to spread messages and create innovative solutions that will help us change our lifestyles to better suit protecting the planet. If you don't feel comfortable using words or actions to help the cause this section is the one for you. By using creative talents to make art that envokes people to think about this world, or signs to help those rally, or even create the tools people need to live a more sustainable life, crafting is a very important part of bending the curve.

Ideas

Create Signs

A great way of crafting for the cause is to create signs for others to hold at rallies. Artwork on signs causes more people to look at the sign and helps to bring the message across. Whether it is digital art, a painted picture, or artfully drawn words, sign design is an important part of getting the message across to others.

Create Art

Creating artwork is another great way to craft a change. By creating work that provokes the mind to think about what the future could become you are able to show others the importance of acting today.

Create Videos

A great way of getting the information out there is by creating a video. Cinematography can artfully combine the facts needed for credability, as well as the imagery needed to make someone care about the topic. By producing videos for youtube or social media you can help the movement by getting the correct facts out to the population.
